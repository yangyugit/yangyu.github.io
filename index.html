Bio

I am a Post Doc at CSE, UC San Diego, advised by Professor Zhongjie Wang. My research focuses on developing sample-efficient machine learning algorithms, with applications in sequential modeling, time-series analysis, natural language processing.

I received my B.S. degree in CS with honors from Chu Kochen Honors College, Zhejiang University.
